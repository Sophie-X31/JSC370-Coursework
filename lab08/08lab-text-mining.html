Question 1: What specialties do we have?

We can use count() from dplyr to figure out how many different medical specialties are in the data. Are these categories related? overlapping? evenly distributed? Make a bar plot.

Answer: The data isn’t evenly distributed, most of the transcriptions are from the surgeries. Also, there aren’t much relation between categories, e.g. surgery and neurosurgery is only somewhat related. The categories seem like distinct specialties, and there isn’t a lot of overlapping.
